Favorite Cases

  • Marbury v. Madison (1803): Established the principle of judicial review.
  • Tinker v. Des Moines (1969): Affirmed students' rights to free speech in schools.
  • New York Times Co. v. United States (1971): Protected the freedom of the press under the First Amendment.
